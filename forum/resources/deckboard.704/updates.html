Version 3.0.0 (Release Candidate 2)

  • Dec 18, 2023

Advance Board

An advance board have four sections you can customize. Three types of section can be chosen from, Button Grid, Sliders, and Twitch Chat.
  • Button Grid, show the standard button grid up to 4 x 3 size;
  • Sliders section introduces new two types of inputs: Sliders and Wheels. Just like macro buttons, you can customize them as well;
  • Twitch Chat, just the name implies, show the Twitch chat feed of your favorite Twitch stream.

Voicemod Integration​

The connection between Deckboard and Voicemod will be established automatically. The macros included in this version:
  • Change Voice Filter
  • Play Sound
  • Toggle "Hear myself"
  • Toggle Voice Changer

New Features / UI Updates​

  • Select scene as preview for OBS Studio's "Select Scene" action while in Studio mode;
  • Group all apps and accounts into one single popup menu;

Fixes​

  • [Linux] Removal of required libnode64 and libicu66 dependencies;

Important Notes​

Several new features are only available in client app version 3.0.0 or above that is currently in beta (Android only). You can participate in beta program straight from Google Play
