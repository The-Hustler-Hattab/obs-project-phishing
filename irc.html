IRC Chat

The majority of the OBS community and support volunteers have shifted over to the official OBS Discord.

You can still use our IRC community chat to get help from volunteers and other OBS users if you prefer. Please do not ask "Can anyone help me?" or "Is anyone here?". It may seem impolite, but it's best to ask your question straight away with as much detail as possible. A copy of your OBS log file will be very useful. See getting help on IRC for more guidelines.

Please keep in mind that the people in the chat are community volunteers and other OBS users. No one is obliged to help you and spamming or a bad attitude may get you kicked.

When entering the chat, you will be placed into the OBS community chat channel automatically. There is no need to join other channels on the network as they are not related to OBS.
